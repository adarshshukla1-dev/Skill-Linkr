Privacy Policy

Welcome to Skill Linkr . This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have. This is a concise, developer-friendly version designed for immediate use. Please consult a lawyer to produce a final version tailored to your jurisdiction.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, phone (optional), college/company name, role (student/company), profile details you add.
  • Project data: titles, descriptions, skills required, deadlines, attachments submitted by companies or students.
  • Authentication data: password hashes or OAuth tokens (we never store plain-text passwords).
  • Usage data: logs, analytics, IP address, device & browser metadata for improving the service and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and profile.
  • To enable project posting, discovery, and communication between students and companies.
  • To send transactional emails (password reset, notifications) and occasional product updates (you can opt out of marketing emails).
  • To improve and secure the platform (bug fixing, analytics, abuse detection).

3. Legal Basis & Retention

We process personal data based on your consent and our legitimate interests (running and improving the product). We retain account information for as long as your account exists and usage/analytics data for up to 24 months unless otherwise required.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, email delivery) under contract with us.
  • When required by law (court orders) or to protect our rights or users' safety.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (HTTPS, salted password hashing, access controls). However, no online service can be 100% secure — please avoid sharing highly sensitive personal information on the platform.

6. Your Choices

You can update or delete your profile data from account settings. For marketing emails, use the unsubscribe link in emails or contact us directly.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies for session management and basic analytics. You can block cookies via your browser but some features might stop working.

8. Contact

If you have questions or requests about your personal data, email us at adarshshuklakota18@gmail.com. For legal requests, include a clear description and verification of identity.
